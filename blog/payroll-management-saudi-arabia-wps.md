Payroll in Saudi Arabia is more than cutting checks. It's WPS file generation, GOSI contributions, housing allowances, and complex end-of-service calculations. Getting it wrong means regulatory problems and unhappy employees.

For employers across Riyadh, Jeddah, and the Kingdom, compliant payroll processing is essential.

## Saudi Payroll Requirements

### Wage Protection System (WPS)

Ministry of Human Resources mandate:

- Salary payments through approved channels
- Monthly WPS file submission
- Documented payment evidence
- Regulatory monitoring

### GOSI (Social Insurance)

Mandatory contributions:

- Saudi employee contributions
- Employer contributions
- Occupational hazard insurance
- Proper registration

### End of Service Benefits

Gratuity calculations:

- Service period calculation
- Applicable salary
- Resignation vs. termination rates
- Legal formula compliance

### Allowance Structure

Common Saudi salary structure:

- Basic salary
- Housing allowance
- Transportation allowance
- Other allowances
- Total package consideration
