## UAE Statutory Benefits

End of service gratuity represents one of the most significant employee benefits under UAE labor law. The calculation basis follows a defined formula where employees receive 21 days of salary per year for the first five years of service, then 30 days per year for service beyond five years. Total gratuity is capped at two years' total salary regardless of length of service.

Qualifying conditions determine eligibility for gratuity. Employees must complete a minimum of one year of continuous service to qualify for any gratuity. Resignation versus termination affects the percentage of gratuity employees receive, with terminated employees generally receiving full entitlement while resigned employees receive graduated amounts based on service length. Limited versus unlimited contract distinctions may affect calculations. Recent labor law updates have modified some provisions that employers must understand and apply correctly.

Annual leave entitlements provide paid time off for all employees. The standard entitlement is 30 calendar days per year after completing one year of service. Pro-rata calculation applies for employees with less than one full year. Accumulation rules govern how much leave can carry forward between years. Encashment provisions address payment for unused leave under specific circumstances.

Leave pay calculations must include appropriate compensation components. Basic salary is always included. The treatment of allowances varies and requires careful attention to ensure correct payment.

Sick leave provides income protection during illness. The entitlement structure provides the first 15 days at full pay, the next 30 days at half pay, and an additional 15 days at no pay. Medical certificate requirements apply to qualify for paid sick leave. Notification obligations require employees to inform employers of illness in a timely manner. Approval processes ensure appropriate documentation and authorization.

Maternity leave supports working mothers with 60 days total leave, of which 45 days are at full pay and 15 days at half pay. Nursing breaks after returning to work provide additional support during the early months.

Other entitlements include public holidays as defined by government announcement, Hajj leave available once per employment, bereavement leave for family deaths, and study leave where company policy provides for it.

## Employee Categories and Benefit Variations

Unlimited contracts represent the most common employment type in the UAE. Open-ended duration provides ongoing employment without fixed end date. Gratuity calculation follows standard formulas. Termination provisions specify notice requirements and compensation. Notice periods apply to both employer and employee when ending the relationship.

Limited contracts establish fixed-term employment. Specific duration defines the employment period. Renewal provisions govern how contracts may continue. Early termination rules address departure before contract end, which may carry financial implications. Gratuity adjustments may apply differently than for unlimited contracts.

Part-time employees present unique benefit challenges. Pro-rata benefits scale entitlements to reflect reduced hours. Minimum entitlements establish floors that apply regardless of schedule. Leave calculation must reflect actual working patterns. Gratuity application follows specific rules for part-time arrangements.
