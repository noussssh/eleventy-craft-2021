An employee completes five years of service and asks about gratuity. Another questions annual leave balance. A third inquires about air ticket entitlement. HR needs accurate answers backed by proper records.

For UAE employers, benefit management is both a legal obligation and employee expectation. Getting it wrong creates disputes; getting it right builds trust.

## UAE Statutory Benefits

### End of Service Gratuity

Legal entitlement:

**Calculation Basis**
- 21 days salary per year (first 5 years)
- 30 days salary per year (after 5 years)
- Maximum 2 years' total salary

**Qualifying Conditions**
- Minimum 1 year service
- Resignation vs. termination rates
- Limited vs. unlimited contracts
- New labor law updates

### Annual Leave

Paid time off:

**Entitlement**
- 30 calendar days per year
- Pro-rata for partial year
- Accumulation rules
- Encashment provisions

**Leave Pay**
- Basic salary included
- Allowances consideration
- Calculation timing

### Sick Leave

Medical absence:

**Entitlement Structure**
- First 15 days: full pay
- Next 30 days: half pay
- Following 15 days: no pay

**Requirements**
- Medical certificate
- Notification obligation
- Approval process

### Maternity Leave

Working mothers:

- 60 days total leave
- 45 days full pay
- 15 days half pay
- Nursing breaks afterward

### Other Entitlements

Additional benefits:

- Public holidays
- Hajj leave (once per employment)
- Bereavement leave
- Study leave (if applicable)
