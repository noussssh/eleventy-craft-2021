You buy the same items from the same supplier every month. Each time, you create a new purchase order, negotiate terms, and process paperwork. Multiply this across dozens of items and suppliers, and procurement becomes an administrative burden.

There's a better way. Blanket purchase orders - also called standing orders or framework agreements - establish terms once and simplify ongoing purchasing.

## What Is a Blanket Order?

A blanket purchase order is a long-term commitment:

**Agreement**
- Price agreed for a period
- Terms and conditions set
- Quantities estimated
- Delivery flexible

**Execution**
- Call-off orders against agreement
- Simplified ordering
- Pre-negotiated terms apply
- Track against commitment

**Benefits**
- Better pricing through volume
- Reduced administrative work
- Faster order processing
- Predictable costs

## Best Practices

### Right Items for Blankets

Suitable for blanket orders:

- Regular, predictable purchases
- Stable specifications
- Known suppliers
- Material quantities

Not suitable:

- One-time purchases
- Highly variable needs
- Rapidly changing specifications
- Unknown suppliers

### Reasonable Commitments

Set appropriate quantities:

- Based on historical usage
- Allow for variation
- Not overly optimistic
- Achievable for supplier

### Regular Review
