## Dubai Leave Requirements

### Legal Entitlements

Dubai labor law provides:

**Annual Leave**
- 30 days per year (after one year)
- Pro-rata for shorter service
- Accumulation limits

**Sick Leave**
- Paid periods
- Medical certificate requirements
- Extended illness provisions

**Other Leave**
- Maternity leave (60 days)
- Hajj leave (one time)
- Bereavement leave
- Public holidays

### Employer Obligations

Legal requirements:

- Grant entitled leave
- Maintain records
- Pay for leave
- Manage accumulation
